# A Visualization Of Five OF The Best Soccer Strikers

## Introduction
This project analysed the career statistics of Robert Lewandowski, Harry Kane, Luis Suarez, Romelu Lukaku and Karim Benzema. There's also a brief focus on the stats of the players over the last 5 seasons/years.


## Technologies and Languages
*Microsoft Excel
*Microsoft Power BI


## How To Use

In Microsoft Power BI

```
Open and view career-stats.pbix -for the visualization of the total career stats of the players
```

```
Open and view last-5-seasons-career-stats.pbix  -for the visualization of career stats over the previous 5 seasons
```

Alternatively, you can view the pdf compilation of the visualizations.
